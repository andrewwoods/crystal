
US - United States Regular Expressions
================================================================================

ZIP codes ( aka Postal Codes )
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
/^\d{5}([\-]\d{4})?$/


Phone Numbers
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Loose /^(?:\+1\-)?\d{3}-\d{3}-\d{4}$/  Loose
Strict /^(\+?1[\-\s\.])?[2-9]\d\d[\-\s\.][2-9]\d\d[\-\s\.]\d{4}$/
